About Us

Our goals with this project was to allow Mi'kmaq people the ability to experience some of the benefits of artificial intelligence to help facilitate the learning of their language. The sad truth is the expansion of technology has been a major culprit in the steady decline of Mi'kmaq speakers. This was painfully obvious when we went to look for resources on the subject. Our hope is that this may serve as a tool to aid the learning process and show what technology has to offer.
